\hypertarget{prioritization-overview}{%
\subsection{Prioritization Overview}\label{prioritization-overview}}

This is the R Code for the Step 2 of the
\href{https://www.ucsrb.org/prioritization/}{UCSRB Habitat
Prioritization process}. I will endeavor to update the data available on
this page, however the most up-to-date results for Step 2 can be found
on the Prioritization Products Page. For each script, the last update is
listed on the right (e.g.~``2 months ago'').

\hypertarget{reach-and-action-proiritizatoin}{%
\subsection{Reach and Action
Proiritizatoin}\label{reach-and-action-proiritizatoin}}

The RTT decided to use three pathways to develop prioritized reaches and
actions: Habitat Quality pathway, Limiting Factors pathway, and fish
passage pathway. This python tool deals primarily with the Habitat
Quality and Limiting Factors pathway, and the fish passage pathways are
uploaded to the action categories manually. The filters and criteria for
filters (as of October 14th, 2020) are overview in this
\href{https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1dEJ-A_LlW1HkxfIjOfLmjRxS1DRR_nWGOsBlZj5khss/edit?usp=sharing}{google
slides presentation}.
